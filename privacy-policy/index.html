Information We Collect

When you connect your Meta account or use features related to Threads, we may collect and process the following information, depending on the permissions granted by you and approved by Meta:

  • Basic account information, such as your user ID, username, profile name, and profile picture.
  • Access tokens required to connect to Meta APIs.
  • Public Threads content and search results available through Meta APIs.
  • Content metadata, such as post IDs, timestamps, usernames, links, and public engagement information.
  • Technical information required for security, debugging, logging, and API operation.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment card data, government IDs, health data, or private messages.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information only for the following purposes:

  • To provide the app's core functionality.
  • To authenticate users through Meta services.
  • To search, analyze, and display public Threads content based on user-defined keywords or criteria.
  • To send alerts or notifications requested by the user.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, debug errors, and improve the reliability of the app.
  • To comply with Meta Platform Terms, applicable laws, and developer requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user personal information.

We may share limited information only when necessary:

  • With infrastructure providers used to host, operate, or monitor the app.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • When necessary to protect the security, rights, or integrity of the app.

Data Storage and Retention

We store information only for as long as necessary to provide the app's functionality, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, or resolve technical issues.

Access tokens and user-related data may be deleted when they are no longer needed, when a user disconnects the app, or when a valid deletion request is received.

User Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at: dmytrii.lysak@gmail.com

Please include the Meta account or app user information needed to identify your data.

After receiving a valid deletion request, we will delete or anonymize the relevant user data unless we are required to retain certain information by law or for legitimate security reasons.

You may also remove the app from your Meta account settings. If Meta sends us a data deletion request, we will process it according to Meta's requirements.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
